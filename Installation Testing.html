Installation Testing - Mandatory Testing

In addition to Visual Verification, AS/NZS 3000 requires you to perform six Mandatory Tests.

MatchTest description with the equivalent Mandatory Test.

1. A test of the main earthing conductor, protective earthing conductors and equipotential bonds to prove they are continuous and the resistance is as prescribed in AS/NZS 3000.

2. Testing the condition of the insulation on a cable, by applying a voltage between all active conductors and earth.

3. De-energised testing to confirm the correct identification and connection of all active, neutral and earth conductors, to remove hazards of transposition of conductors

4. De-energised test to prove the operation of the circuit operates in the manner intended.

5. Testing to determine the correct operation of circuit protective devices in the prescribed time under fault conditions to prevent a dangerous touch voltage.
This test may be conducted on an energised circuit, with appropriate test equipment and procedures.

6. Test the performance and operation of RCD's.
